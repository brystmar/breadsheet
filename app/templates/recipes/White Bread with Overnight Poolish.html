White Bread with Overnight Poolish

★★★★★

Bread

Prep Time: 14 hr
Cook Time: 1 hr 15 min
Difficulty: Medium
Servings: 2 loaves
Source: Flour Water Salt Yeast

Ingredients

1000 g flour

400 g water

23 g salt

2 g yeast

Description

Overnight poolish recipe

Directions

Start the poolish the night before. Let it sit for 12 to 14 hours.

Mix the final dough. This recipe requires two folds, ideally done in the first half hour after mixing. Cover and let the dough rise for 2 to 3 hours.
